Medical Imaging & DICOM Engineering

DICOM Infrastructure Solutions

Healthcare environments require rock-solid reliability. I architect and build specialized DICOM infrastructure that goes beyond standard off-the-shelf PACS limitations, focusing on routing logic, verified delivery, and data integrity.

Custom DICOM Servers

Development of lightweight, high-performance DICOM nodes with full web-based management interfaces. Deployable on premise or cloud, offering granular control over storage and access.

Library Mastery

Web Viewers
